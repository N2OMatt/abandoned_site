Curitiba and my new Host

Oct 3, 2017


Today is: Tuesday 3, October of 2016 - Curitiba, Paraná.

Intro:

Well after a long, long time... I decided to post again!
I'm in Curitiba (capital of State of Paraná) to get closer of the rest of GoEpik team since our release date is getting closer - Yep we gonna release the 1.0 version in a few days.

About Curitiba:

This is my first time here and I'm really loving the city. While is true that I didn't hang around until now - I arrived Sunday - the city already let a very good impression on me.

The streets are super clean, the people are very educated and willing to help you. Food is "cheap" and where I'm located is very near from anywhere else that I might be interested at first glance.

Well.. I'm loving here ;D and I hope that the next two weeks to be great like now!

About my new Host:

I used to use a shared host from GoDaddy, it was a GNU/Linux machine - if I remember correctly, it was a CentOS.

While it cheap, around R$12/month, the service was very, very disappointing.
I had a ultra limited usage possibilities, since every thing that uses CPU or Disk for more than a couple of seconds were killed.

This clearly doesn't works well for me - While my usage is very low in average there are some times that I need use some CPU time, especially to build the Amazing Cow's projects documentation using doxygen.
So, this is why, both my and Amazing Cow's site was very outdated... I simply can't built them...

Now I decided to move to a VPS hosting (hostinger).
Well I can't say anything but that I'm really amazed in...:

  1. How the hostinger control panel is awesome - Very concise, clean, without anything unneeded...
  2. How is good have unrestricted GNU/Linux machine at your hands...

I would tell here how my experience with the actual systems management is going... but I think that it's a nice subject to another post - and I'll let it for later!

Ready to eat some junk food ;D
