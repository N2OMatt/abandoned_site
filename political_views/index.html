My political views:

Here a quick list of my political / life views. While it's clear that:

  1. No one can summarize all points into a single webpage,
  2. All of the subjects are open to change due new evidences, and
  3. This list can't be ever completed and fully updated
I think that's a good way to express my point of view about many subjects that I think interesting ;D

I'm OPEN TO CHANGE all of the subjects, if you disagree with that I would love to hear you and learn about how your thought is constructed.

  • Science
    • The most important thing that humans had invented. Only by science the knowledge can be acquired in a true way.

  • god
    • I don't believe in any form of god - I respect you by believe, but yet think that you should think more about it, because I think that there's no logical way that you can make up a god and make the reasoning holds up without inconsistencies.

  • Religion
    • While I respect the right of the existence of any religion I'm fully against the concept of its existence. I don't think that it brings nothing valuable for life, in any form.

  • LGBT
    • I fully believe that the LGBT community MUST HAVE THE SAME RIGHTS of the heterosexual community. There's nothing wrong being LGBT, in ANY aspect.

  • Gay Marriage / Children Adoption
    • Really? I'm fully agree with that. In respect about the children - the younglings needs are: Care, Love, Education, a strong moral teaching - the sexual organ of their parents don't enter at this list ;D

  • Education
    • I think that it's the strongest and more important thing that a human being must have. By the education one can reason by himself - And that's the most important thing that a person might have.

  • Social / Economical
    • That's trick... While I'm not a socialist stricto sensu I'm inclined to be considered one. I really believe that we must look for each other in all things imaginable and the socialism is the only thing that I know today that do that (yet it's far from be perfect).

  • Legalization / Decriminalization of drugs
    • We can see cases of success in countries that allowed some drugs to be used by citizens. Holland, Uruguay and USA is the most prominent ones.
    Besides that we can see how effective is the policy of "drugs as public health issue" that some European countries have.
    I can enumerate (and I'll do shortly...) a number of reasons of why I think that Marijuana must be legal and other drugs must be treated as public health issue .

  • Abortion
    • With one exception I think that this is a decision regarding only the woman.

    The only exception is, when the couple decided together to have a baby and later on the other part don't want anymore. I think that those cases need a very deep moral and logical analysis to ensure that the right thing to do is achieved.
